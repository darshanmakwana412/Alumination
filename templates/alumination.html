About Alumination
To quench the thirst of all those who strive to gain the experience and expertise of our esteemed alumni, SARC is here with its biggest and most ostentatious event, our flagship, Alumination 2022. It is a 2 day extravaganza with loads of events lined up for the students so that they can excel in whatever field they wish to start their trail in, whether it be core, non-core or any bizarre field! Turn your dreams and speculations into reality (tagline ghusa denge) by being present here on 15th and 16th october!
